# AdaptGrid AI

Domain: Intelligent Renewable & Smart Energy Systems

Approved Problem Statement (as submitted & selected):

> The Problem: Wasted Renewable Energy & Frequent Power Cuts in Odisha
> AdaptGrid AI – Solving Odisha’s Smart Energy Crisis
>
> - Odisha faces severe heatwaves every summer → frequent grid failures & long power cuts  
> - Rooftop solar owners waste 30-40% of generated energy because there’s no smart way to store or share it  
> - Villages and apartment societies have no real-time visibility or optimization  
> - Result: High electricity bills, massive CO₂ waste, and under-used solar projects across the state

What We Built?
AdaptGrid AI is a real-time smart microgrid dashboard that predicts solar energy production for the next 24 hours and instantly tells users the smartest way to use every kWh (Use now / Store in battery / Send to grid).

It connects individual homes and small societies/colonies by showing live energy flow, savings, and optimization suggestions — helping reduce wastage and power cuts during Odisha’s extreme summers.

Key Features:
- Live 24-hour solar prediction using real weather data
- One-click “Optimize Now” with exact ₹, kWh & CO₂ savings
- Cloudy Day & Heatwave simulation sliders
- Real-time dashboard + Odisha map
- Full production deployment (Docker + Jenkins CI/CD + Kubernetes on Oracle Cloud)

Tech Stack: React.js, FastAPI, scikit-learn, Docker, Jenkins, Kubernetes, Cloud
